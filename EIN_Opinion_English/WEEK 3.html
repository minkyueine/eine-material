Work-Life Balance (워라밸)

Today's Goal: Build an opinion about work-life balance, give reasons, and respond to a different viewpoint.
(오늘의 목표: 워라밸에 대한 의견을 세우고 설명하며, 이유를 제시하고, 다른 관점에 대응하기)

Photo: A person relaxing at home after turning off their work laptop.

1. Warm-up (워밍업)

  • What comes to mind when you hear 'work-life balance'?

    ('워라밸'이라는 말을 들으면 무엇이 떠오르나요?)

  • How often do you think about your work-life balance?

    (자신의 워라밸에 대해 얼마나 자주 생각하시나요?)

  • Do people around you have strong opinions about working overtime?

    (주변 사람들은 야근에 대해 확고한 의견을 가지고 있나요?)

  • Why do you think work-life balance matters today?

    (오늘날 워라밸이 왜 중요하다고 생각하나요?)

2. Opinion Frames (의견 표현 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I think ___ .

    (저는 ~라고 생각합니다.)

  • In my opinion, ___ .

    (제 생각에는, ~입니다.)

  • I agree that ___ .

    (저는 ~라는 것에 동의합니다.)

  • I don't think ___ .

    (저는 ~라고 생각하지 않습니다.)

Extra (Optional)

  • What do you think about ___ ? (~에 대해 어떻게 생각하시나요?)

  • I see your point, but ___ . (무슨 말씀인지 알겠지만, ~)

LESSON 11: Work-Life Balance - Case & Practice

REAL CASE PROMPT A company offers a high salary but requires long hours and weekend work. (한 회사가 높은 연봉을 제안하지만, 긴 근무 시간과 주말 근무를 요구합니다.)

3. Model Dialogue (모범 대화)

Tutor
What do you think about jobs with high salaries but poor work-life balance?(연봉은 높지만 워라밸이 나쁜 직업에 대해 어떻게 생각하시나요?)
Student
I think work-life balance is more important than money.(저는 돈보다 워라밸이 더 중요하다고 생각합니다.)
Tutor
Why do you think that?(왜 그렇게 생각하시나요?)
Student
One reason is that people easily get burned out if they don't rest.(한 가지 이유는 쉬지 않으면 사람들이 쉽게 번아웃되기 때문입니다.)
Tutor
Some people say working hard while you are young is necessary. What do you think?(어떤 사람들은 젊을 때 열심히 일하는 것이 필요하다고 말합니다. 어떻게 생각하시나요?)
Student
I see their point, but you also need time for family and health.(그들의 관점도 이해하지만, 가족과 건강을 위한 시간도 필요합니다.)
Tutor
So what would you choose personally?(그럼 개인적으로는 무엇을 선택하시겠어요?)
Student
If I had to choose, I would choose a job with a good work-life balance.(만약 선택해야 한다면, 저는 워라밸이 좋은 직업을 선택하겠습니다.)

4. Guided Practice (의견 만들기 연습)

Opinion Builder (의견 구성하기)

  1. What is your opinion about work-life balance? (워라밸에 대한 당신의 의견은 무엇인가요?)
  2. What is the main reason for your opinion? (그 의견의 주된 이유는 무엇인가요?)
  3. What would someone with the opposite opinion say? (반대 의견을 가진 사람은 뭐라고 말할까요?)
  1. In my opinion, work-life balance is ___________________________________ .
  2. One advantage of having free time is ___________________________________ .
  3. One drawback of working too much is ___________________________________ .
  4. Some people think ___________________, but I think ___________________ .
  5. If I had to choose between 'High salary' and 'More free time', I would choose ___________________ because ___________________ .

6. Talk More

  • Does your current job/study give you good balance?

    (현재의 직장이나 학업은 워라밸이 좋은 편인가요?)

  • Is it possible to be very successful without working overtime?

    (야근 없이 매우 성공하는 것이 가능할까요?)

LESSON 12: Healthy Lifestyle - Case & Practice

REAL CASE PROMPT A friend decides to only eat healthy, organic food, but complains that it is too expensive and time-consuming. (한 친구가 건강한 유기농 음식만 먹기로 결심했지만, 너무 비싸고 시간이 많이 든다고 불평합니다.)

3. Model Dialogue (모범 대화)

Tutor
What do you think about strict healthy diets?(엄격한 건강 식단에 대해 어떻게 생각하시나요?)
Student
I think eating healthy is important, but it shouldn't be stressful.(건강하게 먹는 것은 중요하지만, 스트레스를 받아서는 안 된다고 생각합니다.)
Tutor
Why do you think that?(왜 그렇게 생각하시나요?)
Student
One reason is that healthy food is often expensive and takes time to prepare.(한 가지 이유는 건강한 음식이 주로 비싸고 준비하는 데 시간이 걸리기 때문입니다.)
Tutor
Some people say it is worth the cost to prevent future illnesses. What do you think?(어떤 사람들은 미래의 질병을 예방하기 위해 그 비용을 지불할 가치가 있다고 말합니다. 어떻게 생각하시나요?)
Student
I understand that view, but people need affordable and convenient options too.(그 관점은 이해하지만, 사람들에게는 저렴하고 편리한 선택지도 필요합니다.)
Tutor
So what would you choose personally?(그럼 개인적으로는 무엇을 선택하시겠어요?)
Student
If I had to choose, I would choose a balanced diet over a strict one.(만약 선택해야 한다면, 저는 엄격한 식단보다는 균형 잡힌 식단을 선택하겠습니다.)

4. Guided Practice (의견 만들기 연습)

Opinion Builder (의견 구성하기)

  2. What is the main reason for your opinion? (그 의견의 주된 이유는 무엇인가요?)
  3. What would someone with the opposite opinion say? (반대 의견을 가진 사람은 뭐라고 말할까요?)
  1. In my opinion, eating healthy is ___________________________________ .
  2. One advantage of healthy food is ___________________________________ .
  3. One drawback of strict diets is ___________________________________ .
  4. Some people think ___________________, but I think ___________________ .
  5. If I had to choose between 'Eat only healthy food' and 'Eat what you want but in moderation', I would choose ___________________ because ___________________ .
