Immigration Questions (입국 심사)

Today's Goal: Answer basic immigration questions calmly and clearly.
(오늘의 목표: 방문 목적, 체류 기간, 숙소 등 입국 심사 질문에 침착하게 대답하기)

Photo: A traveler showing their passport to an immigration officer.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Have you ever felt nervous at immigration?

    (입국 심사대에서 긴장한 적이 있나요?)

  • What is the purpose of your next trip?

    (당신의 다음 여행 목적은 무엇인가요?)

  • Have you ever been asked many questions at immigration?

    (입국 심사에서 많은 질문을 받아본 적이 있나요?)

  • Do you prepare documents carefully before a trip?

    (여행 전 서류를 꼼꼼하게 준비하는 편인가요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I'm here for vacation.

    (휴가차 왔습니다.)

  • I'll stay for two weeks.

    (2주 동안 머물 예정입니다.)

  • I'm staying at ___.

    (저는 ___에 머물 것입니다.)

  • Here is my reservation.

    (제 예약 내역이 여기 있습니다.)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • I have a return ticket. (돌아가는 항공권(귀국편)을 가지고 있습니다.)

  • I'm visiting friends in ___. (___에 있는 친구들을 방문할 예정입니다.)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Some countries use e-gates, but officers may still ask questions.

  • Keep answers short and clear. Smile and stay calm.

  • If asked for proof, you can show a hotel booking or return ticket.

LESSON 6: Immigration Questions - Wrap-up & Assignment

6. Talk More

  • Do you enjoy traveling? Why?(여행하는 것을 즐기시나요? 이유는요?)

  • Do you prefer short trips or long trips?(짧은 여행과 긴 여행 중 어느 것을 선호하시나요?)

  • What makes a trip 'good' for you?(당신에게 '좋은' 여행이란 무엇인가요?)

  • What makes border control feel stressful?(입국 심사(국경 통제)가 스트레스로 느껴지는 이유는 무엇일까요?)

  • How do you organize your travel documents?(여행 서류들을 어떻게 정리하시나요?)

Tutor Tip

• Remind the student to answer in full sentences (not single words).

• Practice the 3 core questions (purpose / duration / accommodation) multiple times.

• For higher-level students, ask one extra question: 'What cities will you visit?'.

ASSIGNMENT

Homework

Write 3-4 sentences: purpose, duration, accommodation (include address if possible).

(방문 목적, 체류 기간, 숙소에 대해 3~4문장으로 적어보세요. 가능하면 주소도 포함하세요.)

Example:

I'm here for vacation.
I'll stay for ten days.
I'm staying at a hotel near the city center.

Currency Exchange (환전소 이용하기)

Today's Goal: Exchange money and ask about exchange rate, fees, and small bills.
(오늘의 목표: 돈을 환전하고 환율, 수수료, 소액 지폐에 대해 질문하기)

Photo: A person exchanging currency or holding cash at an exchange booth.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Do you usually use cash or card when traveling?

    (여행할 때 주로 현금을 사용하나요, 카드를 사용하나요?)

  • Have you ever exchanged money at the airport?

    (공항에서 환전해 본 적이 있나요?)

  • Do you usually bring cash when you travel?

    (여행 갈 때 주로 현금을 챙겨가나요?)

  • Have you compared exchange rates before?

    (이전에 환율을 비교해 본 적이 있나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I'd like to exchange ___.

    (___을/를 환전하고 싶습니다.)

  • What is the exchange rate?

    (환율이 어떻게 되나요?)

  • Are there any fees?

    (수수료가 있나요?)

  • Could I get small bills, please?

    (소액 지폐로 받을 수 있을까요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Can I exchange this amount? (이 금액을 환전할 수 있나요?)

  • Can I get some coins as well? (동전도 좀 받을 수 있을까요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Airport exchange booths often have worse rates - banks can be cheaper.

  • Some countries mainly use cards, but cash is still useful for small shops.

  • Ask for small bills if you need to pay for buses, tips, or street food.

6. Talk More

  • Do you track your spending when traveling?(여행할 때 지출 내역을 기록하시나요?)

Buying a SIM Card (유심 카드 구매하기)

Today's Goal: Buy a prepaid SIM and choose a data plan.
(오늘의 목표: 선불 유심을 구매하고 데이터 요금제 선택하기)

Photo: Someone looking at their mobile phone to check connectivity.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • How much mobile data do you use per month?

    (한 달에 모바일 데이터를 얼마나 사용하시나요?)

  • Do you prefer prepaid or contract plans?

    (선불 요금제와 약정 요금제 중 무엇을 선호하시나요?)

  • What do you use your phone for most while traveling?

    (여행할 때 휴대폰을 주로 어떤 용도로 가장 많이 사용하시나요?)

  • Would you rather buy a SIM card or use roaming?

    (유심 카드를 구매하시겠어요, 아니면 데이터 로밍을 사용하시겠어요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I'd like to buy a prepaid SIM card.

    (선불 유심 카드를 구매하고 싶습니다.)

  • How much data is included?

    (데이터는 얼마나 포함되어 있나요?)

  • How much does it cost?

    (가격은 얼마인가요?)

  • Could you help me activate it?

    (개통하는 것 좀 도와주시겠어요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Do you have a cheaper plan? (더 저렴한 요금제가 있나요?)

  • Is hotspot included in this plan? (이 요금제에 핫스팟이 포함되어 있나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • In some countries, you must register your SIM with a passport/ID.

  • Prepaid plans are easier for short trips.

  • Ask staff to activate it if you're unsure about phone settings.

Taking a Taxi / Uber (택시/우버 타기)

Today's Goal: Tell a driver your destination and ask about time and cost.
(오늘의 목표: 기사에게 목적지를 말하고 소요 시간과 요금 묻기)

Photo: A view from inside a taxi driving through a city.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Do you usually take taxis or public transportation?

    (주로 택시를 타시나요, 아니면 대중교통을 이용하시나요?)

  • Have you ever used Uber or a rideshare app?

    (우버나 승차 공유 앱을 사용해 본 적이 있나요?)

  • Do you trust taxi drivers easily?

    (택시 기사님을 쉽게 신뢰하는 편인가요?)

  • Do you prefer taxis or trains from the airport?

    (공항에서 이동할 때 택시와 기차 중 무엇을 선호하시나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • Could you take me to this address, please?

    (이 주소로 가주시겠어요?)

  • About how long will it take?

    (시간이 대략 얼마나 걸릴까요?)

  • How much will it cost?

    (요금은 얼마나 나올까요?)

  • Could you drop me off here, please?

    (여기서 내려주시겠어요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Could you follow the GPS, please? (내비게이션(GPS)을 따라가 주시겠어요?)

  • Could we avoid the toll road, please? (유료 도로는 피해서 가주실 수 있나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Tipping rules vary by country - some places tip drivers, some don't.

  • If you're unsure, show the address on your phone.

  • Some cities have toll roads - ask if you want to avoid them.

Asking for Directions (길 묻기)

Today's Goal: Ask for directions and understand simple route instructions.
(오늘의 목표: 길을 묻고 간단한 경로 안내 이해하기)

Photo: A traveler pointing at a map and asking someone for directions.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Have you ever gotten lost in a foreign country?

    (외국에서 길을 잃어본 적이 있나요?)

  • What do you usually do when you are lost?

    (길을 잃었을 때 보통 어떻게 하시나요?)

  • How do you usually find your way in a new place?

    (새로운 장소에서 보통 어떻게 길을 찾으시나요?)

  • Are you comfortable asking strangers for help?

    (낯선 사람에게 도움을 요청하는 것이 편하신가요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • Excuse me, could you help me?

    (실례합니다만, 좀 도와주시겠어요?)

  • How do I get to ___?

    (___에 어떻게 가나요?)

  • Go straight and turn left/right.

    (직진해서 왼쪽/오른쪽으로 도세요.)

  • Is it far from here?

    (여기서 먼가요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • How long does it take to walk? (걸어서 얼마나 걸리나요?)

  • Is there a landmark I can look for? (제가 찾아볼 수 있는 랜드마크(표시)가 있나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • People often give directions using landmarks (bank, station, mall).

  • If you don't understand, ask: 'Could you repeat that slowly?'

  • Confirm by showing your phone map.
