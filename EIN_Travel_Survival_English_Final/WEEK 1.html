Booking a Flight (항공편 예약하기)

Today's Goal: Ask about flights, prices, and seat options when booking a flight.
(오늘의 목표: 항공편 예약 시 항공편, 가격, 좌석 옵션에 대해 묻기)

Photo: A person looking at flight options online or a view of a plane.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Have you ever booked a flight online?

    (온라인으로 항공편을 예약해 본 적이 있나요?)

  • Which country would you like to visit next?

    (다음으로 어느 나라를 방문하고 싶으신가요?)

  • How often do you travel each year?

    (매년 얼마나 자주 여행을 가시나요?)

  • What is the longest flight you have taken?

    (지금까지 타본 가장 긴 비행은 몇 시간이었나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I'd like to book a flight to ___.

    (___로 가는 항공편을 예약하고 싶습니다.)

  • When would you like to travel?

    (언제 여행하고 싶으신가요?)

  • How much does the ticket cost?

    (티켓 가격은 얼마인가요?)

  • Is it a direct flight?

    (직항편인가요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Are there any cheaper flights? (더 저렴한 항공편이 있나요?)

  • Does the price include taxes and baggage? (이 가격에 세금과 수하물이 포함되어 있나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Online booking is common, but calling is still useful for changes.

  • Some airlines charge extra for checked baggage and seat selection - ask first.

  • In the US/Canada, 'carry-on' is common. In the UK/AU, 'cabin bag' is also used.

  • Do you enjoy traveling or do you prefer staying at home?(여행하는 것을 즐기시나요, 아니면 집에 있는 것을 선호하시나요?)

Choosing Seats & Baggage (좌석 및 수하물 선택하기)

Today's Goal: Choose a seat and ask about baggage allowance and weight limits.
(오늘의 목표: 좌석을 선택하고 수하물 허용량 및 무게 제한에 대해 질문하기)

Photo: Luggage on a scale at an airport check-in counter.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Do you prefer a window seat or an aisle seat?

    (창가 좌석과 통로 좌석 중 어느 것을 선호하시나요?)

  • Do you usually travel with a lot of luggage?

    (여행할 때 짐을 많이 챙기는 편인가요?)

  • How many bags do you usually bring on a trip?

    (여행 갈 때 보통 가방을 몇 개 가져가나요?)

  • What do you always keep in your carry-on bag?

    (기내 수하물 가방에 항상 챙기는 물건은 무엇인가요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I prefer a window seat.

    (저는 창가 좌석을 선호합니다.)

  • I prefer an aisle seat.

    (저는 통로 좌석을 선호합니다.)

  • How many bags can I check in?

    (위탁 수하물은 몇 개까지 가능한가요?)

  • What is the weight limit?

    (무게 제한은 얼마인가요?)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Is carry-on luggage allowed? (기내 수하물이 허용되나요?)

  • Is there an extra fee for baggage? (수하물에 대한 추가 요금이 있나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Carry-on size rules can be strict - measure your bag if unsure.

  • Budget airlines may charge for checked bags (and sometimes carry-ons).

  • 'Baggage allowance' is a common term worldwide.

Airport Check-in (공항 체크인)

Today's Goal: Check in at the airport and respond to basic questions from airline staff.
(오늘의 목표: 공항에서 체크인하고 항공사 직원의 기본 질문에 대답하기)

Photo: A traveler handing over a passport at the check-in desk.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • How early do you usually arrive at the airport?

    (공항에 보통 얼마나 일찍 도착하시나요?)

  • Have you ever used a self check-in kiosk?

    (셀프 체크인 키오스크를 이용해 본 적이 있나요?)

  • Do you prefer self check-in or speaking to staff?

    (셀프 체크인과 직원에게 말하는 것 중 어느 것을 선호하시나요?)

  • Have you ever had a baggage problem?

    (수하물과 관련된 문제를 겪어본 적이 있나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • I'd like to check in for my flight.

    (탑승 수속(체크인)을 하고 싶습니다.)

  • Here is my passport.

    (제 여권 여기 있습니다.)

  • How many bags are you checking in?

    (위탁 수하물은 몇 개인가요?)

  • Here is your boarding pass.

    (탑승권 여기 있습니다.)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Could I change my seat? (좌석을 변경할 수 있을까요?)

  • What time does boarding start? (탑승은 몇 시에 시작하나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Many airports offer self check-in kiosks, but staff can help if there is a problem.

  • For some routes, you may need to show a visa or onward ticket at check-in.

  • In the US, you may be asked: 'Did you pack your bags yourself?'.

Airport Security (공항 보안 검색)

Today's Goal: Understand security instructions and respond clearly at the airport.
(오늘의 목표: 보안 검색대에서 지시 사항을 이해하고 명확하게 대답하기)

Photo: Bags going through an x-ray scanner belt at airport security.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Do airport security checks make you nervous?

    (공항 보안 검색이 당신을 긴장하게 만드나요?)

  • What items do you usually carry in your carry-on bag?

    (기내 수하물 가방에 주로 어떤 물건을 챙기나요?)

  • Have you ever been stopped at airport security?

    (공항 보안 검색대에서 제지당한 적이 있나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • Please put your bag on the belt.

    (가방을 벨트 위에 올려주세요.)

  • Take off your jacket.

    (재킷을 벗어주세요.)

  • Do you have any liquids?

    (액체류를 가지고 계신가요?)

  • Walk through the scanner.

    (스캐너를 통과해 주세요.)

Upgrade (Optional)

  • Do I need to remove my laptop? (노트북을 꺼내야 하나요?)

  • Is this allowed in my carry-on? (이것을 기내에 들고 타도 되나요?)

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Liquid rules are often 100 ml per container in many airports.

  • Some airports ask you to take laptops/tablets out of your bag.

  • In some countries, you may need to remove shoes or belts - follow the signs.

Boarding the Plane (비행기 탑승하기)

Today's Goal: Find your gate, confirm your flight, and board the plane smoothly.
(오늘의 목표: 탑승구를 찾고 비행편을 확인하며 순조롭게 탑승하기)

Photo: Passengers walking through the gate or a view out the terminal window.

1. Warm-up (워밍업 질문)

  • Do you like flying? Why or why not?

    (비행하는 것을 좋아하시나요? 왜죠?)

  • What do you usually do during long flights?

    (장거리 비행 중에는 주로 무엇을 하나요?)

  • Do you like window seats or aisle seats more?

    (창가 좌석과 통로 좌석 중 어느 것을 더 좋아하시나요?)

  • What do you usually do after boarding a plane?

    (비행기에 탑승한 후 주로 무엇을 하나요?)

2. Speaking Patterns (핵심 패턴)

Core (Must-use)

  • Where is gate 15?

    (15번 게이트는 어디에 있나요?)

  • Is this the correct gate for flight 204?

Real-life Travel Tip

  • Many airlines board by groups (Group 1, 2, 3).

  • Some airports announce boarding by row numbers or zones.

  • If you miss boarding, go to the gate counter immediately.
